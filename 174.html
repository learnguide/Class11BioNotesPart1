Meiosis II
Meiosis II is the second meiotic division. It is similar to mitosis, but the genetic results are different. It results in
the formation of four haploid cells from the two haploid cells produced in meiosis I.
The four main steps of Meiosis II are as follows-
Prophase II, Metaphase II, Anaphase II, and Telophase II.
