Anaphase
It is the shortest stage of the cell cycle. During this phase, the replicated chromosomes move apart and daughter
chromatids move to the opposite poles. Chromosomes are maximally condensed during late anaphase. The
chromosomes appear Y- shaped as they move to the opposite poles.
