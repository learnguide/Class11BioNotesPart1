Viroids
:
They are the smallest structures known that are capable of infection. They consist only the nucleic acid without
the protein coat.
Lichens:
They are symbiotic associations of algae and fungi. The algal partner is autotrophic and synthesizes and provides
food. The fungal partner offers protection and shelter.
