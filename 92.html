Corolla
The second whorl of the flower lying above the calyx. Each member is called a petal. Petals with similar
shape and size are symmetrical. Petals which differ is size and shape are asymmetrical. When petals
are free they are called polypetalous whereas when the petals are fused they are called
gamosepalous.
