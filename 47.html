4.2.2 PHYLUM COELENTERATA
Animals belonging to the phylum coelenterata are commonly called coelenterates or Cnidarians. They are
more advanced than sponges in having true tissues.
It includes about 9000 species.
They are aquatic. Most of them are marine.
