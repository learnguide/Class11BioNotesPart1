Permanent tissues
•
The cells that are differentiated from meristems and are specialized in their structure and
function constitute permanent tissues. These cells do not generally divide further.
•
Permanent tissues that are made up of cells that are similar in structure and function are
called simple tissues.
•
Permanent tissues made up of different types of cells are called complex tissues.
Simple tissues
•
A tissue consisting only one type of cells.
•
They are parenchyma, collenchyma and sclerenchyma.
