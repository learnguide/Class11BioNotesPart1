SUB-PHYLA UROCHORDATA:
Notochord present only in tail of larval stage.
Body is covered by test or tunic made up of tunicin
Exclusively marine
Retrogressive metamorphosis
Hermaphrodite
e.g.,
Ascidia, Salpa, Doliolium
.
SUBPHYLUM CEPHALOCHORDATA:
Segmented notochord is persistent extending from head to tail.
The body contains a sac like pharynx with gill slits both for filter feeding and respiration.
Exclusively marine
Fish-like
No definite coelom
Sexes are separate
E.g.
Branchiostoma (Amphioxus or Lancelet)
