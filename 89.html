Cyathium
Bracts or involucre fuse to form a cup shaped structure on the margins of which secretory glands are
found. The central portion of the cup-shaped structure contain female flowers which mature earlier
and emerge out of the cup due to the pedicel growth. They are surrounded by small male pedicellate
flowers. Male flowers towards the centre mature earlier than those present on the periphery.
It is seen in Euphorbiaceae famile like
Euphorbia, Poinsettia, Pedilanthus.
